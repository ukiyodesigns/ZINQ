About ZINQ

ZINQ delivers creative, flexible solutions to unmet corporate financing requirements. With a core focus in secured strategic corporate debt and targeting undercapitalized niches of the market, ZINQ aims to generate consistent risk adjusted returns.

ZINQ seeks a partnership approach with entrepreneurs and management that creates unique solutions to otherwise intractable capital requirements.

Investment Focus

ZINQ strategic venture debt capital is structured to meet specific corporate finance objectives of client companies:

  • Senior Secured Loans (Asset Based, Cash Flow, Enterprise Value)
  • Second Lien/Mezzanine and Structured Equity Financing
  • Debtor in Possession and Exit Financing
  • Unfunded commitments and synthetic Letters of Credit
  • Short or long dated maturities, flexible covenants

ZINQ seeks to provide venture debt solutions for otherwise difficult to finance situations:

  • Tight timeframes and high complexity
  • Management buy-outs and unsponsored/pre-sponsored opportunities
  • Multi-constituent restructurings
  • Previously unbanked or under banked companies
  • Non-traditional assets
